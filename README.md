## Metrics Explained
â— **RevPAR (Revenue Per Available Room):** Assesses a hotel's financial performance by calculating the revenue generated per room available for sale.  
â— **ADR (Average Daily Rate):** Calculates the average price or rate at which hotel rooms are sold on a given day.  
â— **SRS (Sellable Room Nights):** Represents the number of room nights a hotel can sell as part of its strategic revenue management approach.  
â— **DSRN (Daily Sellable Room Nights):** Helps identify specific issues like room blocking or maintenance.  
â— **Realization:** Calculated as URN (Utilized Room Nights) divided by BRN (Booked Room Nights), where BRN includes URN, no-shows, and cancellations.
